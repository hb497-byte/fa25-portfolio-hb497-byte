In this project, I worked in a group with 4 peers to create a client outline and pitch to solve spotted lanternfly (SLF) contamination in grape harvest at vineyards for clients at Cornell CALS Extension, E&J Gallo Winery, and National Grape. We proposed constructing a strainer that could be used post-harvest to separate the SLF from the grapes. Our client outline and pitch included a description and diagram of our proposed protoype, potential risks that accompanied this plan, and questions we hoped to ask our clients in order to gain feedback on our design.
